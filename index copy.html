Yes — signing up is completely free for both students and employers. Students browse and apply to jobs at no cost. Employers only pay a small service fee when they successfully hire someone.

After registering, you upload a photo of your university student card. Our Gemini AI reads the card automatically and verifies your enrollment. The process takes under a minute and you only need to do it once.

Once a job is marked complete, the funds held in escrow by Chargily are released to your Talib-Awn wallet immediately. From there you can withdraw to Edahabia, CCP, or BaridiMob — no foreign bank required.

Talib-Awn is focused exclusively on part-time jobs — think cashiering, warehouse shifts, event staffing, tutoring, delivery, and similar roles with fixed hours. All listings are screened to fit around a student schedule.

Because all payments go through our escrow system, no money moves until both parties confirm the job is done. If a dispute arises, our support team reviews the case and mediates a fair resolution before releasing any funds.
